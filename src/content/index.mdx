# Welcome to the Stratpoint Engineering Hub

The Engineering Hub is the single source of truth for how we build software at Stratpoint Technologies. It captures our principles, golden paths, best practices, and onboarding guides across every engineering domain.

## What This Hub Is For

- **Consistency** — shared standards that cut across teams and projects
- **Velocity** — proven patterns and golden paths so you spend time solving problems, not reinventing structure
- **Quality** — checklists, review guidelines, and automated gates that raise the bar across the board
- **Growth** — onboarding guides that get new engineers productive fast

## Quick Start

1. Read the [Engineering Principles](/engineering-principles) first — they apply to everyone.
2. Find your domain and read the **Golden Path** guide for your stack.
3. Bookmark the **Code Review Checklist** for your domain.
4. Work through the **Onboarding Guide** if you are new to the team.
